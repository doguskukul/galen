Domestic POCT Industry Leader
Guangzhou Wondfo Biotech Co., Ltd., headquartered in Guangzhou science city, has been focusing on the R&D, production, sales and service of point-of-care testing (POCT) products and providing customers with professional rapid diagnosis and chronic disease management solutions since founded in 1992.

Wondfo now has more than 3000 employees and a wide range of products for the rapid identification of cardiovascular diseases, inflammation, tumor, infectious diseases, drug abuse, pregnancy, and so on, widely sold to 140+ countries and regions.
